Purpose

About

The 2025-2026 APSA Northeast Regional Meeting will bring together current physician-scientist trainees as well as undergraduate students interested in pursuing a career in medicine and research. For information on other APSA regional meetings, please visit the APSA website.

Target Audience

This conference is designed for undergraduate and postbac students interested in pursuing a physician-scientist career, current MD/DO-PhD and MD/DO students, residents and fellows engaged in research, early-career physician-scientists and established physician-scientists interested in mentoring the next generation.

Travel and Accommodation Information

Getting to the University of Rochester

The meeting will be held in Flaum Atrium at the University of Rochester School of Medicine and Dentistry (URSMD), located at 415 Elmwood Ave, Rochester NY, 14642.

By Air

Consider flying to the Frederick Douglass Greater Rochester International Airport (ROC) (1200 Brooks Ave, Rochester, NY 14624), located a few miles from URSMD. It is about a 10 minute car ride or 40 minute bus ride to Flaum Atrium at the URSMD.

By Train

Amtrak trains from Boston, New York City, and Toronto stop at the Louise M. Slaughter station (320 Central Ave, Rochester, NY 14605). The station is a 10 minute car ride or 30 minute bus ride to Flaum Atrium at the URSMD.

By Intercity Bus

Buses from Boston, New York City, Toronto, Buffalo, and Syracuse stop at various locations throughout the city (within a 10 minute car ride from URSMD).

By Car and Parking

URSMD is easily accessible by car. Several free parking options will be available. The closest parking lot is Lot 8 (MRB) which is right outside Flaum Atrium (< 1 minute walk). After registration, we will send you a QR code that will provide you entry to this lot. Please see map. We will also have overflow parking at College Town which is a 10 minute walk from Flaum Atrium.

Hotel Accommodation

The closest hotel to the University of Rochester School of Medicine and Dentistry is the Hilton Garden Inn, which is about a 10 minute walk from Flaum Atrium. For additional hotels in the area, please see this document.

Accessibility

The University of Rochester is committed to accessibility. All conference venues and recommended accommodations are ADA-compliant. If you require any specific accommodations, please contact us at mstprochester@gmail.com for any assistance
